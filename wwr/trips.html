Our White Water Adventures

Choose Your Rapids

Memorable Journeys for All Levels.

Nile Rafting Travels offers various levels of rafting adventures on the legendary River Nile. From high-grade adrenaline surges to scenic, relaxed family floats, we have the perfect package to make your vacation spectacular.

Adventures Packages

Bujagali Half-Day Blast

Difficulty: Grade 3 (Moderate)

Duration: 3-4 Hours

Perfect for beginners and groups seeking great excitement without committing to a full day. You will experience three massive rapids and enjoy stunning views of the surrounding riverbank wildlife between runs.

Includes: Safety briefings, midday snacks, and professional photography captures of your run.

The Wild Nile Full-Day Challenge

Difficulty: Grade 5 (Extreme Adrenaline)

Duration: 6-7 Hours

For ultimate thrill-seekers! Test your limits against the powerful waves of the Jinja rapids. Our elite guides will navigate you safely through the most iconic, towering whitewater runs in Africa.

Includes: Hot riverside buffet lunch, cold refreshments, round-trip transport from Jinja, and complete gear rental.

Family Float & Scenic Safari

Difficulty: Grade 1-2 (Easy/Relaxed)

Duration: 3 Hours

Bring the children and grandparents along for a magical cruise on peaceful, scenic bypasses. Swim in warm, gentle pools, spot over 50 species of tropical birds, and admire local fishing villages.

Includes: Family-sized life jackets, safety guides, and refreshing tropical fruit platters.
